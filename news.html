MADISON MUSTANGS DEFEAT LEXINGTON IN BEST OF THE WEST SEMIFINAL

Madison Academic High School defeats Lexington 4-1 in the Best of the West Semifinal game. Muhammed Elsebae scores two goals past Lexington while Adam Joyner and Jonathan Timby each get one to send Madison through to the 2016 Best of the West Final.

THE MUSTANGS WIN THE 2016 BEST OF THE WEST TOURNAMENT!!!
